Predicting Emotions of Marathon Runners from Pictures

Facial expressions are the most effective way of non-verbal communication. It provides information about emotional state and intentions. I am interested in long distance running and this project discusses an application of emotion detection from facial expressions: predicting emotional state of marathon runners based on their pictures. The details of the project can be found here: Capstone Pitch

Energy Star Score Analysis

This project discusses Energy Star scores of buildings from different perspectives by examining the data provided by the City of Seattle's Building Energy Benchmarking and Reporting Program. The dataset summarizes the important features of the buildings as well as their energy consumption and greenhouse gas emission information. The details of the project can be found here: Energy Star Score Analysis

Estimating the Accidents

The first section of this project analyzes the trends of motor vehicle collisions in NYC in between 2012-2018 and clusters the most common collision intersections by using the data provided by NYC Open Data. The second part classifies the collisions involve injuries or deaths. The details of the project can be found here: Vehicle Collison Trends in NYC
